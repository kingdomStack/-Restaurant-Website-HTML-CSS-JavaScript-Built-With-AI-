Our Philosophy

At Élysée, we believe dining is more than sustenance—it is an experience that engages all senses. Our philosophy is rooted in honoring French culinary traditions while embracing modern techniques and global influences.

Executive Chef Laurent Dubois and his team meticulously source ingredients from local purveyors and sustainable farms, ensuring each dish tells a story of quality, seasonality, and respect for nature.

Our sommelier-curated wine list features over 300 selections, with an emphasis on small-production French vineyards and New World discoveries that complement our ever-evolving menu.

Our Chef

Executive Chef Laurent Dubois brings over two decades of culinary expertise to Élysée. Born and trained in Lyon, France's gastronomic capital, Chef Dubois honed his craft under three-Michelin-starred chefs before bringing his vision to North America.

His approach is defined by technical precision, respect for ingredients, and a passion for creating memorable dining experiences. Each dish is a balance of texture, temperature, and flavor, designed to surprise and delight.

"Great cuisine should tell a story—of place, of season, of the hands that crafted it. At Élysée, we write that story anew each day."

– Laurent Dubois
